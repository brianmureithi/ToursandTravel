8 Days Seychelles Family Holiday

The Seychelles is an island country, spanning an archipelago of 115 islands in the Indian Ocean. We can book absolutely any hotel or lodge you wish, but have listed a few of our favourites. With a population of 84,000, this tropical paradise has the smallest population of any African country. The islands are all divided into categories such as granitic islands, coral sand cays, coral islands, Amirantes group, Farquhar group, Aldabra group


 

Your Itinerary

Day 1: Welcome to the Seychelles

Upon your family's arrival to the Seychelles, you'll board a scheduled flight for the island of Praslin - the Seychelles second biggest island. Once on Praslin, a representative from the Coco de Mer Hotel will meet you and transfer you to the hotel. Spend the rest of the day exploring this wonderful accommodation.

Day 2-4: Relaxing on White Sands on Praslin

Situated on over 200 acres, the Coco de Mer has plenty of space for your family to spread out and enjoy the spectacular views and activities. Relax around the two magnificent pools that overlook the Indian Ocean, head down to beach and enjoy a restful sleep in a hammock, or your family can take the complimentary shuttle to the Anze Lazio beach for the day. Indulge your sense of adventure for Creole cuisine at the Hibiscus Restaurant that serves up international and Creole style dishes every evening. There's also a children's menu for the little ones, as well as vegetarian meals can be provided.

Day 5: Flying back to Mahe

Today you'll head to the airport for a flight back to Mahe where a representative of the Constance Ephelia Resort will greet you and take your family to the resort.

Day 6-7: Forget Your Worries in the Seychelles

Spend the next 3 days enjoying this stunning resort as you head out on a sea kayak, or book a treatment at the Spa Village, or just relax by the turquoise waters of the Indian Ocean, or the pool - the Ephelia has 4 of them.

Kids can be entertained at the Mini Club - a multifunctional, supervised area for ages 4-12 years. Activities, both indoor and outdoor, are organised daily by trained staff to keep them busy.

Dinners may end up being a complicated decision-making process as the Resort has 5 unique restaurants for your family to choose from. Each offers its own menu, including kid's menus, and is individually designed and decorated.

Accommodation: Ephelia Resort: - Luxurious resort on a beautiful beach on the island of Mahe in the Seychelles.

Day 8: Tour Ends on Mahe

Your tour ends after your transfer to the Mahe airport
