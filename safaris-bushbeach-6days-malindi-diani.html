6 Days Malindi Diani Holiday

Malindi is a town on Malindi Bay at the mouth of the Galana River, lying on the Indian Ocean coast of Kenya. It is 120 kilometres northeast of Mombasa. It is the capital of the Malindi District. The nearby Watamu resort and Gedi Ruins are south of Malindi.


 

Your Itinerary

Day 1: Nairobi – Malindi

Leave Nairobi for Malindi to arrive at your hotel in the afternoon. Check in your rooms and relax you may take an afternoon siesta or swim and relax by the poolside or lounge. Dinner and overnight stay at the Hotel. Meals: Dinner

Day 2: Malindi-North Coast

After breakfast take a morning romantic beach walk. Spend the day enjoying the hotel facilities like the swimming pool or on special request we can organize for you a full day trip to Malindi Marine Park where you spend the day snookering and swimming with the dolphins. . You may take a sunset beach walk before returning to the hotel to freshen up for dinner. You may take a night out at one of the nearby clubs. Meals – Breakfast, Dinner

Day 3: Malindi-Diani

After breakfast, Enjoy part of the morning relaxing by the poolside or swimming on the last day. Check out of the hotel at 11.00am and leave for Malindi- Diani- South coast and have enroute lunch at a stopover of your choice in Malindi town as you shop at Nakumatt Likoni. Arrive in the afternoon, Check in and spend the rest of the day enjoying the sceneries of this most luxurious hotel in south coast. Freshen up and relax in the hotel before dinner is served.

Day 4: Diani-South Coast

After breakfast spend the day enjoying the hotel facilities like the swimming pool or watch movies at the hotel’s theatre, or enjoy a retreat of a lifetime at one of the best health and beauty spas in Africa. On special request we can organize for you a full day trip to the famous Wasini Marine Park where you spend the day snookering and swimming with the dolphins. The Amazing scenery would be ideal for a photo session for memories of this wonderful times .We can also organise other water sports activities with the hotel such as jet skiing or scuba diving, kite surfing. Return in the hotel to freshen up before dinner and overnight stay. Meals: Breakfast, Dinner

Day 5: Diani-South Coast

Spend the days busking under the sun and relaxing in the hotel. After dinner, you may take a night out on your last night at a nearby club. Meals: Breakfast, Dinner

Day 6: Diani-Nairobi

After breakfast, enjoy part of the morning relaxing in the beach or by the poolside swimming on the last day. Check out of the room by 11a.m and embark on your journey back to Nairobi if you are driving back but if you are taking night bus or flight we can organise for a day trip to Shimba hills national park or Malindi city tour. Meals – Breakfast
