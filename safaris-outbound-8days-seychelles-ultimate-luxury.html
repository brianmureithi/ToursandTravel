8 Days Seychelles Ultimate Luxury

This is a perfect location for those wanting absolute luxury, but still yearning for that wild tropical feeling. The Indian Ocean is warm, turquoise and inviting. Diving, snorkeling and deep sea fishing are excellent here, and all of the hotels we recommend offer all of these activities. For something truly unique and wild, visit one of the private islands. For those sailing or yacht entusiasts, island hopping through the azure waters of the Seychellois Indian Ocean, is an idyllic way to spend a couple of weeks.


 

Your Itinerary

Day 1: Welcome to the Seychelles

Arrive Seychelles. Please let us know if you would like assistance with your international air. Our Concierge will meet you and provide you with personal attention. You will be escorted through customs, and then to the helipad for your flight to North Island.

Day 1-4: North Island

Fournights at one of the most amazing properties in the world - North Island. You will be met and taken to your Villa where you will enjoy 4 nights with all meals, alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages included. Your stay also includes: Scuba Diving courses, snorkeling, sea kayaking, shore based fishing, half fishing charters, mountain bikes and your own private "North Island Buggy". North Island is unashamedly aimed at providing the very best in privacy, location, accommodation, services, facilities and experience. The island has only 11 villas, each exquisitely designed to celebrate nature and offer authentic home-away-from-home ambience. North Island offers service from the heart, tailored to suit your needs and preferred lifestyle. North Island: as nature intended.

Day 5: Flying back to Mahe

You return to Mahe by helicopter. You will be met and transferred to Maia.

Day 5-7: Maia

3 nights at Maia in an Ocean View Villa with breakfast and dinner included daily. At Maia you can enjoy Yoga, Qi Gong, Shiatsu Classes and various water sports. Maia - On the southwest coast of Mahé, you will find Anse Louis , a magnificent, secluded beach. Beyond the swaying palms, aquamarine water and opalescent sands lies the magic of Maia, one of the Leading Small Hotels of the World. Each of the spacious villas is strategically positioned for maximum privacy, and all provide breathtaking vistas across the private peninsula and Indian Ocean. The Maia experience is one that exemplifies the best of every aspect of luxury.

Day 8: Tour Ends on Mahe

You will be picked up at your hotel and transferred to the Mahe International Airport in time to connect with your flight departure.
